Grade Calculator

Instructions:

This calculator implements the final weighted average as described in the Regulations.

It uses marks or exemptions for all 12 courses.

Leave fields empty for exemptions (APL) at levels 4 or 5. IMPORTANT EXCEPTION: If you have any exemptions at level 5 AND also had any resits at level 5 you will need to hand-calculate and insert a mark for the exemptions. The mark to be inserted will be the maximum of the pass mark and the simple mean of the other marks obtained at level 5 AT THE FIRST ATTEMPT.

Enter your marks into the form below and then press the calculate button to see your final weighted average.
